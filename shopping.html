Sail into Onboard Shopping Bliss

WELCOME TO OUR

Onboard Shopping

Embark on a journey of retail indulgence as you explore the exquisite onboard shopping experience aboard our luxury cruise ship. From high-end boutiques to charming souvenir shops, we offer a diverse array of shopping options to cater everyone's taste and preference.


Luxury Boutiques

Indulge in high-end fashion and accessories at our luxury boutiques, featuring renowned designer labels and exclusive collections. From elegant evening wear to sophisticated accessories, these boutiques cater to discerning shoppers seeking luxury and style.

Duty-Free Emporium

Our duty-free stores offer an extensive selection of products, including cosmetics, fragrances, spirits, tobacco, confectionery, alcohol, and more. Discover top brands and exclusive items at unbeatable prices, with savings of up to 30% off regular retail prices.



Souvenir Shops

Take home a piece of your cruise experience with unique souvenirs and mementos from our souvenir shops. Choose from a variety of themed gifts, apparel, accessories, and novelty items that capture the essence of your journey and destination.

Art Galleries

Immerse yourself in a world of artistic expression at our onboard art galleries, showcasing a diverse collection of paintings, sculptures, and other works of art. Browse through exhibitions featuring both established and emerging artists, and perhaps even acquire a masterpiece to adorn your home.
