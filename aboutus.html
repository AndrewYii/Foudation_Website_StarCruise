All About Star Cruise

Having called the sea our home for over 80 years, Star Cruise embraces the wonder, discovery, and responsibility it entails. On a Star Cruise voyage, guests embark on unforgettable journeys, explore breathtaking destinations, and experience unparalleled hospitality. Beyond providing exceptional experiences, we empower our guests to make a difference. From our dedicated crew to our innovative engineers and skilled shipbuilders, we are committed to safeguarding the ocean's future through proactive measures today.

Our Cruise

At Star Cruises, our affinity for the ocean spans generations. Each cruise with us is a journey of exploration and indelible memories for every guest. We provide an immersive, enriching, and secure cruise encounter, where you can savor global cuisine, top-tier entertainment, acclaimed family activities, and cutting-edge technology onboard. Select from an array of diverse itineraries crafted to unveil the essence of the world's most captivating cultures, offering a symphony of sights, sounds, and flavors.

Sustainability

Star Cruises is committed to environmental stewardship, employing a multifaceted approach to sustainability. Through stringent practices like waste management and energy-saving initiatives, alongside alternative fuels and advanced wastewater treatment, the company minimizes its ecological footprint. Additionally, it prioritizes destination conservation, partnering with local organizations to protect natural habitats and cultural heritage. Its dedication extends to sustainable sourcing, employee engagement, and community outreach programs, fostering responsible tourism practices.

Our History

Founded in 1934, Star Cruise service has been setting the standard for luxury travel on the high seas ever since.

1934

Leo Star come up an idea for the Star Cruise plan. Development phase begins as Star gathers a team of architects, engineers, and designers to bring his vision to life. Maiden voyage of the first Star Cruise Service ship sets sail, marking the official launch of the company.

1960

Star Cruise Service expands its fleet and route offerings, establishing itself as a leading player in the luxury travel industry

1980

Introduction of innovative amenities and onboard entertainment sets Star Cruise Service apart from competitors

2000

Star Cruise Service becomes a household name, known for its exceptional service and luxurious accommodations

2020

Introduction of cutting-edge technologies and experiences, ensuring that Star Cruise Service remains at the forefront of the industry
