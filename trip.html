First Destination (Langkawi)

Malaysia's 'GEM'

Explore the enchanting island of Langkawi on this captivating shore excursion. From the iconic Sky Bridge to the mysterious mangrove forests, this adventure offers a perfect blend of relaxation and discovery.

Beach Hopping

Explore stunning beaches like Pantai Cenang and Tanjung Rhu.

Mangrove Tours

Discover the island's rich biodiversity on mangrove tours.

Underwater World Langkawi

Visit Southeast Asia's largest aquarium to marvel at marine life.

Trekking & Nature Walks

Explore Langkawi's lush rainforests through hiking trails.

Cable Car Ride

Take a thrilling ride on the Langkawi SkyCab to witness breathtaking views of the island.

Duty-free Shopping

Enjoy shopping for souvenirs, chocolates, alcohol, and electronics at duty-free outlets.

Second Destination (Phuket)

Thailand's Tropical Paradise

Embark on a thrilling Phuket shore excursion and dive into vibrant culture as you explore ancient temples and bustling markets. With rich history and tropical beauty, Phuket promises an unforgettable experience for every traveler.

Elephant sanctuaries

Interact with rescued elephants in ethical sanctuaries where you can feed and bathe them.

Phuket Old Town

Wander through Phuket Old Town, which is adorned with colorful Sino-Portuguese buildings.

Visit Temples

Explore Phuket's cultural heritage by visiting temples like Wat Chalong.

Night Markets

Experience the vibrant atmosphere of Phuket's night markets.

Spa & Wellness

Indulge in relaxation with a spa treatment or yoga session.

Phuket FantaSea

Experience Thailand's rich culture and mythology through a dazzling show at Phuket FantaSea.
