Premiere Restaurants

Experience exquisite dining at our premiere restaurant onboard the cruise, where impeccable service and delectable cuisine merge to create unforgettable culinary moments. All the food will be chagred to the respective restaurants.

Pioneer Palate

🍽 Western Food Restaurant
Deck 3, Main Deck
3:00 p.m. - 10.30 p.m.
Saddle up for a culinary adventure as we serve up sizzling, mesquite-grilled steaks, tender BBQ ribs slathered in tangy sauce, and hearty chili simmered to perfection.

Sakura Seafare

🍣 Japanese Food Restaurant
Deck 8, Promenade
6:00 p.m. - 2:00 a.m.

Yurui Restaurant

🥢 Chinese Food Restaurant
Deck 5, Atrium
12:00 p.m. - 10:00 p.m.

Deck 3, Main Deck
12:00 p.m. - 12:00 a.m.

Deck 8, Promenade
