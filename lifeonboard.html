Discover Exceptional Amenities

Our cruise ship offers a range of exceptional facilities, including rock climbing, a gym, spa, children's program, casino, and sports court, ensuring there's something for everyone to enjoy.

Rock Climbing

Embark on an adventure aboard our cruise ship and discover the thrill of rock climbing! Scale our onboard wall with breathtaking ocean vistas. Ascend 12 meters above deck on our signature Rock Climbing Wall, offering excitement for climbers of all levels. No reservations required - enjoy this exhilarating activity again and again!

Gym

Enjoy individual TVs on cardio machines and auto-counting pulse monitors for an engaging and effective workout. Stay refreshed with free water coolers and towels, making your wellness journey convenient and revitalizing.

Family Time
