Wet and Wild Adventures

Experience the ultimate aquatic thrill with Star Cruise's world-class waterpark facilities. From the largest vortex ride to interactive playgrounds and surf pools, it's wet and wild fun for all!

Typhoon Twister

Prepare for an electrifying rush as you brave the twists, turns, and vortex loops of our Typhoon Twister waterslide, an adrenaline-fueled adventure that propels you through a wild ride of high-speed thrills and exhilarating splashes, promising an unforgettable experience for thrill-seekers seeking the ultimate aquatic adventure.

Water Coaster

Prepare for an adrenaline-pumping adventure on our High-Speed Water Coaster! This thrilling ride takes you on a wild journey filled with steep drops and sharp turns, propelling you at lightning-fast speeds. Perfect for thrill-seekers, this heart-racing experience promises unforgettable moments and an irresistible urge for more. Dive in and let the adventure begin!

Waterplexx 5D

Immerse yourself in a unique multi-sensory adventure at Waterplexx 5D. Feel the rush of wind, mist, and water sprays as you are dazzled by lasers, 3D visuals, and immersive sounds. This thrilling cinematic experience combines motion, light, and water effects to create an unforgettable journey.

High Speed Slide

Get your heart racing on our High-Speed Slide! This exhilarating ride takes you on a wild journey with steep drops and sharp turns, propelling you at incredible speeds. Ideal for adrenaline junkies, this heart-pounding adventure guarantees an unforgettable experience that will leave you wanting more.

Lazy River

Immerse yourself in a unique multi-sensory adventure at Waterplexx 5D. This thrilling cinematic experience combines motion, light, and water effects to create an unforgettable journey. Prepare to be soaked and exhilarated as you embark on this ultimate wet and wild ride!

Reptite Slide

Experience the ultimate adrenaline rush on our Reptite Slide! Brace yourself for an electrifying ride filled with heart-pounding twists, turns, and drops. Feel the rush of wind as you speed down the slide at thrilling speeds, making for an unforgettable adventure that will leave you craving more.
